How do I set up my company email on my mobile device?

To set up your company email on your mobile device, follow these steps: 1. Open the email app on your device. 2. Go to "Add Account" and select "Microsoft Exchange" or "Corporate". 3. Enter your company email address and password. 4. Follow the prompts to complete the setup. For security reasons, enable two-factor authentication after setup.

What do I do if I forget my password?

For password resets, visit the company password portal at reset.company.com. Click on "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions to reset it using your registered email or phone number. If you encounter issues, contact the IT helpdesk at support@company.com or call 1-800-555-1234.

How do I connect to the company VPN?

To connect to the VPN, first install the VPN client from the portal: vpn.company.com. Then: 1. Launch the VPN application. 2. Enter your company credentials. 3. Select the appropriate server location (e.g., US-East). 4. Click "Connect". Ensure you have the latest version of the VPN software installed.

How do I secure my laptop when working remotely?

To secure your laptop while working remotely: 1. Always use the company VPN. 2. Enable the firewall and keep antivirus software updated. 3. Avoid using public Wi-Fi without a VPN. 4. Lock your device when not in use (Ctrl+Alt+Delete on Windows). Contact IT for additional security tools.
