HR Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about HR policies, benefits, documentation, and other employment-related matters.

What documents do I need to submit during HR onboarding?

You will need to submit identification documents (ID/passport), tax forms, educational certificates, proof of address, and bank details for direct deposit. All documents should be submitted through the secure portal in the HR Onboarding section.

When will I receive my first paycheck?

Paychecks are issued on the last working day of each month. If you join mid-month, your first paycheck will be prorated based on your start date. Direct deposits typically appear in your account by end of day.

How do I apply for leave or time off?

Leave requests should be submitted through our HR management system. You can access this system from the employee portal. Please submit your requests at least two weeks in advance for planned leave, except in cases of emergency.

What benefits am I eligible for and when do they start?

Benefits include health insurance, retirement plans, and flexible spending accounts. Health insurance coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date. Retirement benefits are available after completing three months of employment.

How do I update my personal information?

You can update your personal information through the employee profile section of the HR Portal. For major changes like name changes, please contact HR directly as additional documentation may be required.

What is the company's work-from-home policy?

Our company offers flexible work arrangements. Depending on your role, you may be eligible to work remotely 2-3 days per week. Please discuss specific arrangements with your manager and ensure they are documented in the HR system.

When will I receive my company ID card?

Your company ID card will be issued during your first week. You'll receive an email when it's ready for pickup from the security desk. The ID card is required for building access and should be worn visibly while on company premises.

Who do I contact if I have questions about my compensation?

For questions regarding your compensation, please contact your HR Business Partner or the Compensation team at compensation@company.com. You can also schedule a confidential meeting through the HR Portal.
