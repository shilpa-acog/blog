Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by going to the login page and clicking on "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I submit a leave request?

Leave requests can be submitted through the HR portal. Navigate to "Time Off" section and click on "New Request".

Where can I find the company policies?

All company policies are available in the HR section of the intranet. You can also ask your manager for specific policy documents.
