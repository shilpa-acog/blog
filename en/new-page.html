Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

HR Questions

To request time off, log into the HR portal, navigate to "Time Off Requests," and submit your dates. Your manager will receive a notification to approve your request.

Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually in June and December. You'll receive a notification from HR when it's time to complete your self-assessment.

IT Support

You can reset your password by visiting the IT portal and clicking on "Password Reset." Follow the instructions sent to your email to create a new password.

To request new software, submit a ticket through the IT portal with details about the software needed and business justification. Approval from your manager may be required.

General

Meeting rooms can be booked through the Outlook calendar. Select "New Meeting," add participants, and use the "Rooms" feature to find and book an available meeting space.

All company policies are available in the HR section of the intranet. Navigate to Onboarding > HR Policies to access the complete policy documentation.
